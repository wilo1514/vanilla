# Skill: Claim Guard

All public copy must be evidence-led and safe.

Approved wording:
- Single-origin Ecuadorian Vanilla Tahitensis beans
- Certified organic under applicable programs
- Estate-grown
- Estate-cured
- Lot-documented
- Independently analyzed for selected harvests
- Built for professional buyers
- Vanilla. Perfected.

Approved vanillin wording:
Our August 2025 / Harvest 2 sample was independently analyzed and reported at 8,273 ± 847 mg/kg vanillin.

Do not say:
- highest in the world
- best in the world
- guaranteed vanillin
- all lots have the same result
- superior to Madagascar
- superior to Tahiti
- traditional premium origins average a fraction
- carbon neutral
- medicinal
- therapeutic
- health benefits
- pesticide free
- chemical free

If the content contains risky wording, return:
NEEDS REVIEW

Every generated email, post, or website section must include:
claims_used: [...]
risk_status: SAFE or NEEDS REVIEW
